checkbox

01. checkbox - 기본 

<div class="form-check">
    <input type="checkbox" id="check-default" class="form-check-input" />
    <label for="check-default" class="form-check-label">기본 체크박스</label>
</div>

02. checkbox - 커스텀 

<div class="form-check form-check-custom">
    <input type="checkbox" id="check-custom" class="form-check-input" />
    <label for="check-custom" class="form-check-label">커스텀 체크박스</label>
</div>
커스텀 체크박스는 기본 구조에 .form-check-custom 클래스를 추가한다.

03. checkbox - 상태별

checkbox - 선택

<div class="form-check form-check-custom">
    <input type="checkbox" id="check-checked" class="form-check-input" checked />
    <label for="check-checked" class="form-check-label">선택 상태</label>
</div>

checkbox - 비활성

<div class="form-check form-check-custom">
    <input type="checkbox" id="check-disabled" class="form-check-input" disabled />
    <label for="check-disabled" class="form-check-label">비활성 상태</label>
</div>

checkbox - 선택 비활성

<div class="form-check form-check-custom">
    <input
        type="checkbox"
        id="check-checked-disabled"
        class="form-check-input"
        checked
        disabled
    />
    <label for="check-checked-disabled" class="form-check-label">선택 비활성 상태</label>
</div>

04. checkbox - 그룹

관심 항목 
<fieldset class="form-fieldset">
    <legend class="form-legend">관심 항목</legend>

    <div class="form-option-group">
        <div class="form-check form-check-custom">
            <input type="checkbox" id="check-group-01" class="form-check-input" />
            <label for="check-group-01" class="form-check-label">옵션 1</label>
        </div>

        <div class="form-check form-check-custom">
            <input type="checkbox" id="check-group-02" class="form-check-input" />
            <label for="check-group-02" class="form-check-label">옵션 2</label>
        </div>

        <div class="form-check form-check-custom">
            <input type="checkbox" id="check-group-03" class="form-check-input" />
            <label for="check-group-03" class="form-check-label">옵션 3</label>
        </div>
    </div>
</fieldset>
동일한 질문에 속하는 체크박스 묶음은 fieldset과 legend를 사용한다.

05. checkbox - 전체 선택 

<div class="form-check form-check-custom form-check-all">
    <input type="checkbox" id="check-all" class="form-check-input js-check-all" />
    <label for="check-all" class="form-check-label">전체 선택</label>
</div>

<div class="form-option-group form-option-group-vertical">
    <div class="form-check form-check-custom">
        <input type="checkbox" id="check-item-01" class="form-check-input js-check-item" />
        <label for="check-item-01" class="form-check-label">항목 1</label>
    </div>

    <div class="form-check form-check-custom">
        <input type="checkbox" id="check-item-02" class="form-check-input js-check-item" />
        <label for="check-item-02" class="form-check-label">항목 2</label>
    </div>
</div>
전체 선택은 JavaScript로 제어한다.
개별 항목을 모두 선택하면 전체 선택도 함께 선택되며, 일부만 선택된 경우에는 중간 선택 상태로 표시된다.
